FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are all products genuinely handmade?

Yes, 100%. Every single item listed on our store is completely designed, hand-thrown, hand-forged, or hand-woven by independent master artisans. No automated assembly lines or high-volume industrial machines are ever used.

How do you support and source your artisans?

We source directly by visiting regional craft collectives and home workshops. We practice fair trade policies by guaranteeing our partners set their own pricing, paying ethical wages upfront, and investing a portion of store profits into local workshop expansions.

Do you ship worldwide and package sustainably?

Yes, we ship globally! To protect fragile ceramics and items, we utilize eco-friendly, biodegradable honeycombed paper wrapping, compostable organic cornstarch peanuts, and recycled double-walled cardboard boxes.

Can I request customized dimensions or engravings?

Absolutely! Items marked as "Customizable" (like our Engraved Leather Wallet) can be directly customized by filling out checkout prompt notes. For fully custom designs on pottery or woodwork, please email us directly through the contact page form.
